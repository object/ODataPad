Privacy Policy

ODataPad is committed to maintaining your confidence and trust, and recognizes your right to keep personal information private. We maintain the following privacy policy to protect personal information you provide online.

Basic Privacy Policy

The application collects no personal information about you at anytime.

Information Collected from Application Usage

The only information collected is the definition of OData services, and it will only be used by the application to connect to the respective OData services and retrieve its metadata. Any stored information will be used solely for the purpose of performing these tasks.

No Release of Information to Third Parties

The information collected by the application will not be released to third parties except where it is legally required, such as in the investigation of a criminal offence, and in compliance with a search warrant or subpoena.
