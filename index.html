Welcome to ODataPad!

ODataPad is a free tool that can be used to inspect OData services metadata. It comes with registered metadata from 10 well-known providers of OData services, and users can extend or modify the service collection.

ODataPad can do the following:

  • Retrieve metadata information from OData services
  • Show information about OData service collections, properties and relations

Authors and Contributors

ODataPad is maintained by Vagif Abilov as an Open Source project, so anyone can contribute in it's further development.
